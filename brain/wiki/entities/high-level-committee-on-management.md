# High-Level Committee on Management

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[brain/raw/UN-CEB-on-AI|UN-CEB-on-AI]]

## Description
The High-Level Committee on Management (HLCM) is a key subsidiary body of the [[entities/United-Nations-System-Chief-Executives-Board-for-Coordination|United Nations System Chief Executives Board for Coordination]] that focuses on administrative and management policy coherence across the UN system. It works in conjunction with the [[entities/High-level-Committee-on-Programmes|High-Level Committee on Programmes]] to address the operational and ethical integration of [[concepts/Artificial-Intelligence|Artificial Intelligence]] within UN organizations. The committee is responsible for establishing task forces to develop internal policies, such as frameworks for the responsible use of AI and the identification of technical capacity-sharing mechanisms. Its initiatives are essential for transforming high-level strategic goals into actionable administrative standards for UN staff.
