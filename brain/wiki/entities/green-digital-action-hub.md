# Green Digital Action Hub

## Basic Information
- Type: project
- Source: [[sources/news-itu-cop30_gda-hub-2025|news-itu-cop30_gda-hub-2025]]

## Description
The Green Digital Action Hub (GDA Hub) is a cooperation platform launched at [[entities/cop30|COP30]] to accelerate digital innovation for climate action. It serves as a central point for coordinating sustainability efforts across the tech sector and government policy. The hub provides tools, expertise, and data to monitor environmental footprints and support the Global South in their transition. This initiative builds directly on the framework established by the [[concepts/green-digital-action|Green Digital Action]] partnership initiated at COP28.
