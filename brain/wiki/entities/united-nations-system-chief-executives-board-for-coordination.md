# United Nations System Chief Executives Board for Coordination

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[brain/raw/UN-CEB-on-AI|UN-CEB-on-AI]]

## Description
The [[entities/United Nations System Chief Executives Board for Coordination|United Nations System Chief Executives Board for Coordination]] (CEB) is the longest-standing and highest-level coordination forum of the United Nations system. It provides strategic direction and coordination among the various organizations, funds, and programs of the UN. In the context of [[concepts/Artificial Intelligence|Artificial Intelligence]], the CEB has played a central role in establishing system-wide policies, frameworks, and ethical guidelines to ensure a coherent approach to technological innovation. The board is chaired by the UN Secretary-General and works closely with its subsidiary machinery, including the [[entities/High-level Committee on Programmes|High-level Committee on Programmes]].
