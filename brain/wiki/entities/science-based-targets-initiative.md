# Science Based Targets initiative

## Basic Information
- Type: project
- Source: [[sources/itu-t-l-1470-greening-digital|ITU-T L.1470 (Greening Digital)]]

## Description
The Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) is a collaborative framework that helps companies set greenhouse gas emission reduction targets aligned with the latest climate science. The initiative provides the necessary criteria and guidance for organizations to transition to a net-zero economy. Specifically, the standard [[sources/itu-t-l-1470-greening-digital|ITU-T L.1470 (Greening Digital)]] was developed in collaboration with the SBTi, the [[entities/gsma|GSMA]], and [[entities/ge-si|GeSI]] to provide sectoral decarbonization pathways for the ICT industry. This ensures that emissions trajectories are consistent with the objective of limiting global warming to 1.5°C in line with broader [[concepts/climate-action|climate-action]] goals.
