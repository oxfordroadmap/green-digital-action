# United Nations Institute for Training and Research

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/news-itu-cop30_gda-hub-2025|news-itu-cop30_gda-hub-2025]]

## Description
The United Nations Institute for Training and Research (UNITAR) is a specialized training agency within the United Nations system. It plays a critical role in the [[entities/green-digital-action-hub|Green Digital Action Hub]] by bridging science, policy, and capacity development for sustainable digital transformation. The institute's objective is to provide data-driven insights and hands-on training to help countries accelerate responsible digital innovation that supports climate goals and circular economy transitions. Through its work, UNITAR contributes to global efforts in achieving [[concepts/sustainable-development-goals|Sustainable Development Goals]].
