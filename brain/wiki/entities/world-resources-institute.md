# World Resources Institute

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/itu-t-l-1480-greening-by-digital|ITU-T L.1480 (Greening by Digital)]]

## Description
The World Resources Institute is a global research organization focused on sustainability, [[concepts/climate-action|climate action]], and economic development. Within [[sources/itu-t-l-1480-greening-by-digital|ITU-T L.1480]], the organization is recognized for its technical contributions toward estimating the comparative emission impacts of products. Its research provides essential guidance on avoiding double counting in emission accounting, which significantly influences the standard's approach to reporting and broader [[concepts/greening-by-digital|Greening by Digital]] strategies.
