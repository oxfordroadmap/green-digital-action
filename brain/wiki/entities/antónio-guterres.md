# António Guterres

## Basic Information
- Type: person
- Source: [[brain/raw/UN-CEB-on-AI|UN-CEB-on-AI]]

## Description
António Guterres serves as the Secretary-General of the United Nations. He has been a vocal proponent of harnessing [[concepts/artificial-intelligence|Artificial Intelligence]] for the 'common good' while urging caution regarding risks like bias and data privacy. Under his chairmanship, the [[entities/united-nations-system-chief-executives-board-for-coordination|United Nations System Chief Executives Board for Coordination]] (CEB) has prioritized the governance of frontier technologies to ensure the UN system effectively supports Member States.
