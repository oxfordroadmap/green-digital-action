# French Agency for Ecological Transition

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/itu-t-l-1480-greening-by-digital|ITU-T L.1480 (Greening by Digital)]]

## Description
The French Agency for Ecological Transition (ADEME) is a public entity that promotes and supports the implementation of ecological transition policies in France. In the context of [[sources/itu-t-l-1480-greening-by-digital|ITU-T L.1480]], ADEME is cited for its foundational work in developing methodologies for assessing greenhouse gas emission impacts and reduction actions. Its frameworks are used as a basis for the standard's consequential approach to evaluating the environmental consequences of deploying ICT solutions. Through its research and operational guidance, the agency contributes significantly to global [[concepts/climate-action|Climate Action]] standards.
