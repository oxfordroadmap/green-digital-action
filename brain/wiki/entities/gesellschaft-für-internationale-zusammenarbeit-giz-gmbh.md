# Gesellschaft für internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ) GmbH

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/news-itu-cop30_gda-hub-2025|news-itu-cop30_gda-hub-2025]]

## Description
Gesellschaft für internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ) GmbH is a German development agency that operates globally to implement sustainable development projects. As a key partner in the [[entities/green-digital-action-hub|Green Digital Action Hub]], GIZ focuses on bridging climate and digital agendas to achieve climate justice. The organization provides scalable digital climate solutions and works to integrate global strategy with country-level implementation. Its efforts align with broader international objectives, including the pursuit of the [[concepts/sustainable-development-goals|Sustainable Development Goals]].
