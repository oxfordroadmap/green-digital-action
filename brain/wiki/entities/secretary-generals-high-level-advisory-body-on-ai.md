# Secretary-General's High-level Advisory Body on AI

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[brain/raw/UN-CEB-on-AI|UN-CEB-on-AI]]

## Description
The High-level Advisory Body on AI is a specialized group convened to provide expert insights and guidance on the governance of [[concepts/ai-governance|AI Governance]]. It serves as a central hub for international deliberations, integrating diverse perspectives from various sectors to address the rapid evolution of technology. The body works in conjunction with [[entities/united-nations-system-chief-executives-board-for-coordination|United Nations System Chief Executives Board for Coordination]] system-wide contributions, such as the UN System White Paper on AI Governance, to inform policy development at a global scale. Its primary role is to assist the international community in understanding the impacts of AI and recommending inclusive, values-based governance mechanisms.
