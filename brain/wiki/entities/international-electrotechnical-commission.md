# International Electrotechnical Commission

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/statement-gdc-wsc_march-2024|statement-gdc-wsc_march-2024]]

## Description
The International Electrotechnical Commission is a global, not-for-profit membership organization that develops international standards for all electrical, electronic, and related technologies. As a member of the [[entities/world-standards-cooperation|World Standards Cooperation]], it plays a critical role in defining technical specifications that ensure the safety, efficiency, and interoperability of electronic systems. It contributes to digital cooperation by providing a consensus-based framework for industry and government stakeholders.
