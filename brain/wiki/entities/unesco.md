# UNESCO

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[brain/raw/UN-CEB-on-AI|UN-CEB-on-AI]]

## Description
UNESCO is a specialized UN agency that led the collaborative efforts to develop a global ethical framework for artificial intelligence. It was responsible for the 'Recommendation on the Ethics of Artificial Intelligence,' which serves as a foundational document for the UN system's own ethical AI principles. The organization co-led the [[entities/inter-agency-working-group-on-ai|Inter-agency Working Group on AI]] to ensure that ethical considerations remain central to the adoption of new technologies across the UN. Its work contributes significantly to the broader landscape of [[concepts/digital-cooperation|Digital Cooperation]].
