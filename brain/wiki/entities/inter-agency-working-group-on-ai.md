# Inter-agency Working Group on AI

## Basic Information
- Type: project
- Source: [[sources/un-ceb-on-ai|un-ceb-on-ai]]

## Description
The Inter-agency Working Group on AI (IAWG-AI) was a time-bound project established by the [[entities/high-level-committee-on-programmes|High-level Committee on Programmes]] to coordinate AI-related initiatives throughout the UN system. Co-led by the [[entities/international-telecommunication-union|International Telecommunication Union]] and [[entities/unesco|UNESCO]], the group unified nearly 50 UN entities to foster policy coherence. The group was responsible for developing foundational documentation, such as the White Paper on AI Governance. Its mandate concluded in October 2025 following the successful delivery of its core objectives.
