# International Energy Agency

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/itu-t-l-1470-greening-digital|ITU-T L.1470 (Greening Digital)]]

## Description
The International Energy Agency is an intergovernmental organization that provides analysis and data on global energy trends and policies. In the context of [[sources/itu-t-l-1470-greening-digital|ITU-T L.1470]], the IEA is a primary reference for energy scenarios, particularly through its Energy Technology Perspectives (ETP) reports. These scenarios help define the baseline electricity usage and projected carbon emission intensities necessary for modeling the ICT sector's decarbonization pathways. Its data supports the normative electricity budget approach adopted by the [[entities/international-telecommunication-union|International Telecommunication Union]] to ensure sector alignment with global [[concepts/climate-action|climate-action]] goals.
