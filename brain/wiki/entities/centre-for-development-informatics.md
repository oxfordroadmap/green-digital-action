# Centre for Development Informatics

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/itu-t-l-1501-adaptation-best-practices|ITU-T L.1501 (Adaptation Best Practices)]]

## Description
The Centre for Development Informatics is an academic research center based at the University of Manchester. It is widely recognized for its scholarly contributions regarding the role of information and communication technologies (ICTs) in international development. The center provides essential conceptual frameworks and policy guidance, particularly focusing on the intersection of [[concepts/greening-by-digital|ICTs]] and climate change adaptation in developing countries. Its research outputs and publications are cited extensively within [[sources/itu-t-l-1501-adaptation-best-practices|ITU-T L.1501]] as a primary methodological resource for practitioners and policymakers.
