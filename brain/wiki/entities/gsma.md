# GSMA

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/itu-t-l-1480-greening-by-digital|ITU-T L.1480 (Greening by Digital)]]

## Description
The GSMA is a global organization representing the interests of mobile operators worldwide. It plays a significant role in developing frameworks for sustainable digital transformation and has been acknowledged in [[sources/itu-t-l-1480-greening-by-digital|ITU-T L.1480]] for its contributions to defining the "enablement effect." This concept describes how mobile communication technologies contribute to carbon emission reductions, serving as a critical reference for practitioners bridging the gap between theoretical lifecycle assessments and real-world business reporting. The GSMA actively collaborates with international bodies to advance [[concepts/climate-action|Climate Action]] within the telecommunications sector.
