# ITU-T Study Group 5

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/itu-t-l-1480-greening-by-digital|itu-t-l-1480-greening-by-digital]]

## Description
ITU-T Study Group 5 is the primary organ within the [[entities/international-telecommunication-union|International Telecommunication Union]] responsible for the development of international standards related to the environment, climate change, and the circular economy. The group oversees the creation and maintenance of L-series recommendations, which include vital methodologies for assessing the environmental impact of information and communication technologies. By coordinating technical and operating questions, the group ensures that climate policies and assessment techniques are standardized globally. Organizations and practitioners applying [[concepts/greening-by-digital|Greening by Digital]] methodologies, such as [[concepts/recommendation-itu-t-l-1480|Recommendation ITU-T L.1480]], are encouraged to submit their reports to the Study Group 5 Secretariat for information and potential verification.
