# Global Enabling Sustainability Initiative

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/itu-t-l-1470-greening-digital|ITU-T L.1470 (Greening Digital)]]

## Description
The Global Enabling Sustainability Initiative (GeSI) is a global partnership of information and communication technology companies and organizations dedicated to sustainability. It has collaborated with the [[entities/international-telecommunication-union|ITU]], [[entities/science-based-targets-initiative|SBTi]], and [[entities/gsma|GSMA]] to develop sectoral decarbonization pathways for the ICT sector. Its involvement ensures that the [[concepts/international-standards|international standards]] reflect both industry-wide technical capabilities and environmental priorities. GeSI provides research and guidance on how digital technologies can drive the transition to a low-carbon economy.
