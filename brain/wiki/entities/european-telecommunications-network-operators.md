# European Telecommunications Network Operators

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/itu-t-l-1470-greening-digital|ITU-T L.1470 (Greening Digital)]]

## Description
The European Telecommunications Network Operators (ETNO) is a prominent trade association representing the interests of major telecommunications operators across Europe. The organization plays a critical role in providing granular data regarding electricity usage and operational carbon emissions for both fixed and mobile network operators. This data is fundamental to validating baseline energy consumption patterns within the ICT sector and facilitates the development of accurate sub-sector decarbonization models. Through its collaborative efforts, ETNO contributes to the broader goals of environmental sustainability in digital infrastructure.
