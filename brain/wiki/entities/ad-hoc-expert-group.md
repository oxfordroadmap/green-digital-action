# Ad Hoc Expert Group

## Basic Information
- Type: other
- Source: [[sources/un-ceb-on-ai|UN-CEB-on-AI]]

## Description
The Ad Hoc Expert Group was a 24-member committee appointed by the Director-General of [[entities/unesco|UNESCO]] to draft the non-binding Recommendation on the ethics of AI. Comprised of experts serving in their personal capacity, the group was responsible for developing the foundational text that eventually guided international consensus on AI ethics. Their work resulted in the first version of the Recommendation published in May 2020. This group acted as a catalyst for ideas, allowing the UN system to align its internal ethical policies with broader global standards regarding the [[concepts/ethics-of-artificial-intelligence|Ethics of Artificial Intelligence]].
