# High-level Committee on Programmes

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[brain/raw/UN-CEB-on-AI|UN-CEB-on-AI]]

## Description
The High-level Committee on Programmes (HLCP) is a formal committee under the [[entities/united-nations-system-chief-executives-board-for-coordination|United Nations System Chief Executives Board for Coordination]] (CEB) responsible for programme policy and operational coordination across the UN system. It serves as the primary driver of the organization's strategic work on frontier issues, including artificial intelligence, the future of work, and data governance. The committee facilitates the development of policy inputs, strategies, and white papers that guide the UN's engagement with emerging technologies. Its work often involves inter-agency collaboration to ensure a coherent approach to global challenges.
