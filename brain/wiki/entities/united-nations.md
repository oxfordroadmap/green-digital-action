# United Nations

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/itu-t-l-1480-greening-by-digital|itu-t-l-1480-greening-by-digital]]

## Description
The United Nations is the primary international organization under whose mandate the [[entities/international-telecommunication-union|International Telecommunication Union]] operates. It provides the global framework, such as the Paris Agreement, which motivates international efforts to limit global temperature rise and achieve Net Zero emissions. The [[entities/international-telecommunication-union|International Telecommunication Union]] serves as the specialized agency of the United Nations responsible for telecommunications and ICT matters, effectively linking technical standards to global [[concepts/climate-action|Climate Action]] goals.
