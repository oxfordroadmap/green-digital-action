# World Bank Group

## Basic Information
- Type: organization
- Source: [[sources/news-itu-cop30_gda-hub-2025|news-itu-cop30_gda-hub-2025]]

## Description
The World Bank Group is a prominent international financial institution providing financial and technical assistance to developing countries. It plays a pivotal role in the [[entities/green-digital-action-hub|Green Digital Action Hub]], working to foster sustainable digital transformation and climate action. By contributing specialized expertise, the institution helps integrate digital solutions into national development and climate strategies. Through collaborative efforts with partners like the [[entities/international-telecommunication-union|International Telecommunication Union]] and the [[entities/gesellschaft-für-internationale-zusammenarbeit-giz-gmbh|Gesellschaft für internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ) GmbH]], the World Bank Group bridges the critical intersection of digital and climate agendas.
